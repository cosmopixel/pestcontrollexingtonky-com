Keeping your Lexington home free from unwanted eight-legged visitors and pesky insects requires a strategic approach. As Lexington's trusted pest control experts, we've compiled proven methods to help you maintain a pest-free environment throughout the year.

Common Household Pests in Lexington Homes

Kentucky's climate creates perfect conditions for various pests, particularly in areas like Masterson Station and central Lexington. Common invaders include:

  • Brown recluse spiders
  • House crickets
  • Carpenter ants
  • American house spiders
  • Silverfish

Preventive Measures for Spider Control

Start with these essential steps to discourage spiders from making your home their habitat:

  • Seal entry points around windows and doors
  • Remove webs regularly
  • Keep outdoor lights off when possible
  • Maintain a clean, clutter-free environment

Natural Pest Prevention Methods

Before considering chemical solutions, try these natural approaches:

  • Use essential oils like peppermint and citrus
  • Place cedar blocks in closets and drawers
  • Maintain a regular cleaning schedule
  • Remove standing water sources

Professional Prevention Strategies

While DIY methods help, professional intervention ensures comprehensive protection. Our residential pest control services include:

  • Thorough property inspection
  • Targeted treatment plans
  • Preventive barrier applications
  • Regular maintenance visits

Year-Round Protection Tips

As discussed in our guide about seasonal pest activity in Lexington, different seasons bring various challenges. Maintain year-round protection by:

  • Scheduling quarterly inspections
  • Maintaining proper drainage
  • Storing firewood away from home
  • Keeping vegetation trimmed

When to Call Professional Help

If you notice any of these signs of pest infestation, it's time to call the experts:

  • Increasing spider populations
  • Visible pest damage
  • Unusual insect activity
  • Persistent problems despite DIY efforts
