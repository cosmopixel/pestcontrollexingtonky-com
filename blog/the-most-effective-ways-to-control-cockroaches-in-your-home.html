Why Cockroaches Are a Serious Problem in Lexington Homes

Cockroaches aren't just unsightly - they pose serious health risks to Kentucky families. These pests can spread bacteria, trigger allergies, and contaminate food. In our humid Kentucky climate, particularly in areas near Winchester and Midway, cockroaches can thrive year-round if left unchecked.

7 Professional Methods for Cockroach Control

1. Seal Entry Points

Inspect and seal these common entry points:

  • Gaps around pipes and utilities
  • Cracks in foundation walls
  • Spaces under doors
  • Torn window screens

2. Eliminate Food Sources

Similar to our approach in ant control, proper food storage is crucial:

  • Store food in airtight containers
  • Clean spills immediately
  • Keep pet food sealed
  • Remove garbage regularly

3. Reduce Moisture

Like silverfish, cockroaches thrive in moist environments:

  • Fix leaking pipes
  • Address basement moisture issues
  • Use dehumidifiers in damp areas

4. Professional Baiting Systems

Our expert technicians use professional-grade baiting systems strategically placed throughout your home.

5. Regular Monitoring

As discussed in our guide about common household pests in Lexington, consistent monitoring is key to prevention.

6. Preventive Treatments

Regular preventive treatments, similar to our mice control services, create a protective barrier around your home.

7. Professional Intervention

Sometimes, DIY methods aren't enough. Our professional pest control team uses integrated pest management techniques for lasting results.

When to Call Professional Pest Control

  • Multiple cockroach sightings
  • Droppings in cabinets or drawers
  • Egg cases
  • Unusual odors
